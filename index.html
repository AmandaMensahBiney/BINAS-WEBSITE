About Binas Prime Enterprise

Founded in 1990, Binas Prime Enterprise specializes in premium snacks, baked goods, and savory treats.

We craft mouth-watering cakes, doughnuts, cookies, turnovers, and more using the finest ingredients.

Beyond delicious treats, we empower women and vulnerable groups with culinary skills training in pastry making and food preparation. Whether you're celebrating an occasion or need catering services, we've got you covered!
